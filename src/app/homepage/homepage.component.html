Welcome! This class focuses on giving MHCID students a deeper understanding of the decisions and practices of the Software Engineers they will work alongside, alongside a bit of a hands-on introduction to programming and software development. We'll cover the strategies that teams use to organize larger projects such as managing data and code, integrating with coding libraries and frameworks, and effectively leveraging AI. We'll also do a shallow dive into programming in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which form the backbone of web and mobile development.
